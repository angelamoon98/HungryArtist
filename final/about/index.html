Our Mission

This website allows local artists to connect to local venues, primarily cafes. This spin on the idea of a "starving artist" is meant to empower artists to share their work. Our project seeks to correct issues for various artists of different backgrounds and interests. It enables artists to showcase their artwork at select cafes, improves venues' foot traffic, and drives increased revenue for both venue and artist.
