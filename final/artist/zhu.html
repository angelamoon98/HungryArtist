Pingyi Zhu

About Pingyi

Pingyi has been an artist for as long as she can remember. When she was younger, she wanted to go to art school, but instead, she found a better balance of disciplines at Duke University. She is currently a junior studying Economics and Math with a minor is Visual Arts. Even though she tends to work primarily in paint, she values versatility in art, so she loves experimenting with different forms. She hopes that versatility will show through the pieces she has created!

Pingyi's work is currently on display at Allday Cafe in Research Triangle Park, NC.
