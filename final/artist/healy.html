Karen Healy

About Karen

Karen Healy is a documentary photographer and artist based in North Carolina. Through portraiture and environmental landscapes, her work explores quiet moments that illuminate the larger story. She has a Certificate in Documentary Arts from the Center for Documentary Studies at Duke University and a B.A. in Communication Studies from the University of Massachusetts. Karen grew up in New England and moved to North Carolina in 1993 with her husband and children. A Palette of Rust and Dreams was inspired by her love of trains.

Karen's work is currently on display at Allday Cafe in Research Triangle Park, NC.
