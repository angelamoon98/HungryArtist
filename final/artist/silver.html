Susie Silver

About Susie

The inspiration for Susieâ€™s imagery, and for the layering process, comes from the natural movement of liquids, rock formations and aerial views of land and water. Pieces are actively layered with media such as ink, watercolor and acrylic, and constructed over time through multiple working sessions. She uses minimal tools in the process of creating pieces often using her hands, fingers and even her breath to guide mediums around the working surface. She focuses on the manipulation of many different media while immersed in the process of creating.

Susie's work is currently on display at Allday Cafe in Research Triangle Park, NC.
