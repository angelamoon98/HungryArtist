HungryArtist

Who We Are

This website allows local artists to connect to local venues, primarily cafes. This spin on the idea of a starving artist is meant to empower artists to share their work, cafes to turn their place into a gallery, and the public to take a break from consuming digital media and appreciate physical art forms. Our project enables artists to showcase their artwork at select cafes, improves venues' foot traffic, and drives increased revenue for both venue and artist. If you've seen a HungryArtist artwork at a cafe, simply navigate this page to learn more about the work!
